About Us

Why Work With Us?

Our lead developer, Shridhara Pavel, believes the best developers are also great teachers. In his free time, he runs a YouTube channel where he breaks down complex topics like CUDA programming and OS internals. This passion for teaching reflects in our work: we build solutions that are not only powerful but also well-documented and easy to understand.

Our team's experience is broad, spanning from low-level machine learning compilers to full-stack web and game development. This versatility means we can see the bigger picture and tackle challenges from different angles, whether your project needs a robust backend, a fluid user interface, or a complex machine learning model. Led by a full-time full-stack engineer and part-time project lead, our team brings both deep technical skills and the leadership needed to see a project through to success.

While we're comfortable across the stack, our real passion is in low-level development. This fascination with what's "under the hood" drives us to write highly efficient, optimized code, which translates into faster, more reliable, and cost-effective applications for our clients.

Meet the Team

"team s" is a collective of passionate and skilled developers, designers, and project managers, hand-picked by our lead, Shridhara Pavel. We are a remote-first team, allowing us to bring together the best talent from around the world. Our collaborative approach ensures that every project benefits from a diverse range of perspectives and expertise, resulting in innovative and robust solutions.

If you're looking for a dedicated partner to build something great, we'd love to hear from you. Feel free to reach out with any contract or freelance opportunities.
